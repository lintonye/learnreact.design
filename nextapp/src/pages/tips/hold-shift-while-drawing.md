FramerXTip (or UX issue?): Holding SHIFT while drawing on oval, you expect to get a circle, but if release shift and mouse at the same time, you'll get an ellipse. Same in Sketch and Figma. üòê

Solution: release the mouse first and hold the shift for a couple more seconds.
